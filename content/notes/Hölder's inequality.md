## Theorem
For any vectors $\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y} \in \mathbb{R}^n$ and for any $p,q \geq 1$ such that $\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} = 1$, it holds that
$$
|\mathbf{x}^\top\mathbf{y}| \leq \sum\limits_{i=1}^n|x_iy_i| \leq \|\mathbf{x}\|_p\|\mathbf{y}\|_q.
$$
